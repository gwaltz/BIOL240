Outline

This project will be an outline of the proecesses and methods organisms use to turn molecules like CO2 and H20 into a usalble format of energy like ATP. We will start at the level of a plant and follow CO2 and its role in photosynthesis. We will then look at how the nectur produced by plants is then used by animals to create energy.

After understanding the ways in which these organisms produce energy, we will then look at how different abiotic and biotic factors may change the energy creation processes. What aspects of each cycle will changein order to sustain the production of energy? Knowing this, we will then look at how we can observe these changes through our environment through experimentation.
