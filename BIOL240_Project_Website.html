Bioenergetics Project

By George Waltz

Timeline

  • 3.5 billion years ago - First life on Earth

    Single-celled organisms appear in the oceans

  • 530 million years ago - Cambrian Explosion

    First complex multicellular organisms appear

  • 65 million years ago - Extinction of Dinosaurs

    Mammals diversify and become dominant land animals

  • 200,000 years ago - Homo Sapiens

    Modern humans evolve in Africa

Organisms

  • Trilobites

    Extinct arthropods that lived in the oceans during the Paleozoic Era

  • Tyrannosaurus Rex

    Large, carnivorous dinosaur that lived during the Late Cretaceous period

  • Humans

    Modern humans that evolved in Africa and spread across the world
